Why Donate Blood?

Every two seconds, someone in need requires a blood transfusion. Blood donation is a simple, safe, and quick process that can greatly impact patients in surgeries, accident recovery, cancer treatment, and more. A healthy person can donate blood every 3 months and help ensure a steady supply in hospitals.

Donating blood not only helps patients survive but also helps your body by stimulating the production of new blood cells, improving cardiovascular health, and reducing harmful iron stores in the body.
